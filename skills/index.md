- [双菱形思考法](double-diamond-research/SKILL.md) - Use when the user says 双菱形思考法, 双菱形, Double Diamond, or Double Diamond Research, or when an open complex research, strategy, product, organization, market, policy, AI adoption, industry, or cross-stakeholder problem must be framed from unclear problem space to evidence-backed decision options.
